CAREER PROFILE

My name is Ruth Kitasi, a certified Data Scientist with a bachelor's degree in Business Information Technology and over four years of experience applying machine learning and analytics to real-world problems. Currently, I work as a Data Executive at Africa Health Business, a healthcare consulting firm, where I design and implement data driven solutions to evaluate internal processes, improve operational efficiency, and support evidence-based strategies that assist clients in exploring and entering the healthcare market in Kenya and across Africa.

My expertise lies in combining quantitative and qualitative data to identify trends, build predictive models, and create interactive dashboards that inform strategic decision-making. My current work in the healthcare industry, alongside previous experience in real estate and microfinance, has given me a unique perspective on how data can solve sector-specific challenges.

Proficient in Python, SQL, Power BI,Looker, Nvivo and advanced Excel, I apply statistical analysis and machine learning techniques to uncover patterns in complex datasets, delivering data-driven solutions that promote growth, innovation, and measurable impact.
